Cookie Policy

Effective Date: October 2025 · Last Updated: October 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how AidHandy Inc. (“AidHandy,” “we,” “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies on aidhandy.com.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store preferences, track usage, and enable site functionality. Similar technologies include pixels and local storage.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Strictly Necessary: essential for login, session continuity, and platform security.
  • Performance: measure site usage and improve reliability.
  • Functional: remember preferences like timezone or language.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party service providers for payments, analytics, or email delivery. These providers may set their own cookies governed by their privacy policies. Examples include:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Postmark, Twilio — email/SMS performance tracking
  • Netlify, Supabase — hosting and analytics

5. Managing Cookies

  • You can adjust browser settings to block or delete cookies. Doing so may limit some functionality.
  • Our website displays a consent banner allowing you to accept or decline non-essential cookies.
  • We do not honor “Do Not Track” signals at this time due to industry standard limitations.
