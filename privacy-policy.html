Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025 · Last Updated: October 2025

AidHandy Inc. (“AidHandy,” “we,” “us”) operates the website and services available at aidhandy.com. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1) Information We Collect

  • Provided by you: name, email, phone number, flight details, preferences, booking metadata; support messages; optional profile info.
  • Payments: limited billing info handled by payment processors (e.g., Stripe). We do not store full card numbers.
  • Automatically: device/browser info, IP, timestamps, and usage analytics (performance and security).
  • From partners: limited data from providers (e.g., flight status APIs) to fulfill bookings.

2) How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve bookings, communications, and support.
  • Send service-related emails/SMS: confirmations, status updates, account notices, flight reminders.
  • Prevent fraud, secure accounts, and comply with law.
  • Operate analytics to improve product reliability and usability.

We do not sell personal data. We do not use phone numbers for marketing without separate consent.

3) SMS and Email Communications (TCPA/Twilio Compliance)

Consent: By providing your phone number on AidHandy.com, you consent to receive service-related SMS communications about bookings and account activity.

  • Message frequency varies based on your activity. Pre-flight reminders are typically sent 6 hours before scheduled departure.
  • Message/data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for assistance. You may also email support@aidhandy.com.
  • No marketing without separate, explicit opt-in.

4) Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use strictly necessary cookies for security and authentication and performance cookies for analytics. You can control cookies through browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect functionality.
